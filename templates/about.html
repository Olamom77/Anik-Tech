About Us

About Anik Tech School

Founded in 2010, Anik Tech School is one of Nigeria's leading technology universities, committed to producing world-class graduates who drive innovation across Africa and beyond.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality technology education that empowers students to solve Africa's most pressing challenges.

Our Vision

To be Africa's most impactful technology university by 2030, producing graduates who lead globally.

Our Values

Excellence, integrity, innovation, and community. We believe technology is a force for good when guided by strong values.

Our History

Starting with just 200 students in 2010, Anik has grown to over 4,200 students across 6 schools and 38 programs.

Meet our Founder

Abdulmalik Abdulgafar

Founder & Proprietor, Anik Tech School

It is my pleasure to welcome you to Anik Tech School. When we opened our doors in 2010, our mission was simple: to give Nigerian and African students access to a world-class technology education that prepares them not just for jobs, but for leadership in an increasingly digital world. Every classroom, lab, and program here is built on that founding belief — that talent is everywhere, and opportunity should be too. I invite you to explore what we've built together, and to imagine the role you could play in shaping the next generation of African innovation.
